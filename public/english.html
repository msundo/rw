The healing

Soul-Healing is a powerful and efficient tool, which transforms energy blockages and old patterns that no longer serve us. This kind of healing happens on soul level and uses information from the Akashic records.

The Akashic records are the field in which all information that has ever been known and all thoughts that have ever been thought leaves an impression. It’s a kind of mass consciousness where all information about all universes is stored. Each time you use your intuition, you receive information from here.

I have been trained to work in these records, and have been given specific guides who assist me in finding the relevant data. An Akashic guide is a kind of entity who protects the archive and also functions as a kind of librarian. I receive the information as symbols, pictures, feelings and words, which enables me to read and understand it.

This makes it possible to receive information about deeply buried energy blockages which we carry with us, often originating from our past lives. These imbalances typically arise because of traumatic experiences which often create negative patterns of thought and behavior (survival mechanisms). The longer we carry them with us, the heavier they become, and the more imbalance they create. These patterns can be tough to get rid of without any help.

As general psychology states, negative patterns wish to be acknowledged and understood before a transformation can occur. When originating from past life experiences, it can be difficult to become aware of them.

This is why it’s very efficient to work with the Akashic records. They give us access to information which help us shed light upon the underlying mechanisms that control us, gain insight into how they came to be and understand why we act the way we do. At the same time, I can make sure the blockages are transformed and healed, allowing the soul to restore its balance and reclaim its lost power.

The session

The Akashic records exist on another plane, outside our time and space. This is where the healing happens, and therefore it doesn’t matter where you physically are in relation to me. Nor is it crucial when I do the actual work. All that is required is that you give me your permission to make a transformation and that I have the opportunity to share the information with you after. I do the work in advance, because I need deep concentration and can easily spend a couple of hours on it.

Together with my guides, I uncover and transform your energy blockages and imbalances. I often get an insight into past lives where the experiences originate from. I also often receive messages that I will pass on to you.

Afterwards we agree on a time for the session, where we can talk it through. It can either be face to face, or it can take place over Messenger, Whatsapp or Skype. All sessions are individual and vary in length and content, but it usually takes about an hour's time.

After a session you can experience after-effects. These can be experienced very differently depending on how sensitive you are, what types of blockages you’ve had and how long you have carried them. Many experience a great relief, increased energy and greater inspiration. Some get tired and need to rest.

You may experience old patterns reappearing while they are being dissolved and leaving your system.

Other symptoms may be temporary illness as a form of cleansing. Some notice a change already when the session is booked. It may also be that you do not really notice anything until some time has passed and you look back at how things have changed since the session.
